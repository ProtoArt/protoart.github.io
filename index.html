ProtoArt

Helping indie game developers create high quality pixel art.

GitHub

We're creating a suite of tools to help indie game developers make pixel art for their games. Soon you'll be able to finish your prototypes faster using our software to generate your spritesheets.

The first step: The sprite compiler.
This tool takes a 3D model and generates a spritesheet for all your different poses and animations. Quickly iterate on your design as you only update a single model rather than every sprite individually.

We've got a proof-of-concept that shows our idea works and we're hard at work making it ready for you to use in your projects. Sign-up below to be notified of our progress!
